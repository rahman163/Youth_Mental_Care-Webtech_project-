Our Services

Diagnosis

Learn more about what makes you special. We treat our patients with utmost care and respect.

We ask basic questions of our patients family, society and health issues. This is crucial to finding out the most proper diagnosis and assign proper treatment by our staff.

One-on-One

Most people have difficulties in every day life. Although some have difficulties to express more than others

We offer one-on-one sessions to enquire our patients day to day life and discover little by little on what kind of obstacles they face in their minds. We also offer consulatation and counselling services to help them face society.

Group Therapy

Have anything to share? Need more motivation or confidence in a group environment?

We allow our patients to meet with each other to better communicate and understand better. We assign our therapists to lead a group on designated session times to ensure peace and calmness within the group environment.

Mental Test

When speaking of tests, people rather get worried or be prepared to get the highest points possible given.

In contrast to that, the main objective of the mental test is to encounter various situations to our patients and test their minds capabilities. We include indoor and outdoor activities in this particular manner.
